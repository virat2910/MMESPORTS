ABOUT MILIMETER ESPORTS

Where Precision Meets Performance

Our Mission

At Milimeter Esports, we believe that greatness is achieved millimeter by millimeter. We are dedicated to building a world-class competitive gaming organization where passion meets precision, and every player has the opportunity to shine on the biggest stages.

With 45+ tournaments hosted and daily scrims running throughout the day, we provide endless competitive opportunities. Our community participates in intense scrimmages every single day, competing for ranking points and striving for excellence.

We foster a culture of excellence through organized T3, T2, and elite-level scrims, high-stakes tournaments with prize pools, and a supportive community that values discipline, respect, and continuous improvement. Every player matters, and every victory counts.
