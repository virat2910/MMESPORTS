Rules & Regulations

01

Team Requirements

Each team must have 4 players minimum and 5 maximum. All players must be verified in Discord with valid Game IDs.

02

Match Format

Battle Royale format with multiple games. Ranking based on final placement and eliminations. Top teams qualify for finals.

03

Fair Play Policy

No cheating, hacking, or use of prohibited equipment. Violations result in immediate disqualification and ban.

04

Prize Distribution

Prizes distributed within 48 hours post tournament. Top 3 teams rewarded. Team captain verification required.

05

Registration Rules

Registration closes 2 hours before tournament. No late entries. Entry fees non-refundable after deadline.

06

Dispute Resolution

All disputes must be reported within 30 minutes of match end. Admin decision is final and binding.

Privacy Policy

At Milimeter Esports, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect: Game IDs, Discord usernames, email addresses, and tournament participation data.

How We Use It: To organize tournaments, distribute prizes, and communicate important updates.

Data Protection: Your information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without consent.
